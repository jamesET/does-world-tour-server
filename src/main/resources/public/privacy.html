Privacy Policy

When accessing the Beer Tour, the application will learn certain information about you.

We collect and store the following information about you:

  • Your email, if you provided one to join, will be used to identify you each time you login to the application. You may be contacted via this address regarding your beer list, account administration tasks such as resetting your password, or promotional info from Doe's Bentonville.
  • Your name, if you provided one to join, will be displayed in the app and may be visible to other users.
  • The name of the domain from which you access the Internet
  • The date and time you access our site

The application will access your location to restrict some features which are available only while visiting Doe's Bentonville. Your location is not stored and will not be shared.

The information collected is for statistical purposes. The Beer Tour may use software programs to create summary statistics, which are used for such purposes as assessing the number of users, what information is of most and least interest, determining technical design specifications, and identifying system performance or problem areas.

For site security purposes and to ensure that this service remains available to all users, Beer Tour uses software programs to monitor network traffic to identify unauthorized attempts to upload or change information, or otherwise cause damage.

The Beer Tour will not obtain personally-identifying information about you when you use the app, unless you choose to provide such information to us, nor will such information be sold or otherwise transferred to unaffiliated third parties without the approval of the user at the time of collection.
