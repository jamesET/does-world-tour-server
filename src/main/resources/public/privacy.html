You may be contacted via this address regarding your beer list, account administration tasks such as resetting your password, or promotional info from Doe's Bentonville.
2. Your name. The name you provide will be displayed in the app and may be visible to other users. 3. The name of the domain from which you access the Internet

The Beer Tour will not obtain personally-identifying information about you when you use the app, unless you choose to provide such information to us, nor will such information be sold or otherwise transferred to unaffiliated third parties without the approval of the user at the time of collection.
